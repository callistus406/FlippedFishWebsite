The SCIN Chronicles

The story of the SCIN follows three heroes—Cool Guy, Dynamic, and Simple—who are torn apart and sent into vastly different dimensions. Simple ends up in a vibrant, silly realm where he reconnects with forgotten memories and begins to question his origins. As he explores, he discovers clues about his past, including a mysterious goop tied to his creation and a figure named Cool Guy who once saved him. This sets the stage for a deeper mystery across the multiverse.
